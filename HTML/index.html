Applied Research

NSCC Applied Research develops practical and innovative solutions for industry's real-world problems. With an expert research team and network of faculty and students from some of our most challenging and cutting-edge programs, we help industry improve their products, enhance processes, and grow their businesses. NSCC has well-equipped facilities which provide us with the tools to contribute to the development of new technologies that we can transfer to our partners. The commercialization of research findings adds value to the communities we serve and contributes to the economic growth of our province.
